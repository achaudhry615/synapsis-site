Frequently asked questions

Does Synapsis AI need an account or internet connection?
No. There are no accounts, and the app's learning features work fully offline. Your content and progress stay on your device.

What data do you collect?
None. Synapsis AI does not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data?
Use Settings → Reset within the app to erase all locally stored content, or delete the app — all data is removed with it, because it only ever existed on your device.

Is the curriculum reviewed?
Curriculum content is currently a draft pending independent external review, and is labeled as such in the app. Do not treat it as a certified or complete curriculum.

Can children use the app?
This release is designed for adults 18+ (parents, teachers, tutors, homeschool educators) preparing learning materials. A child-facing mode is not available in this release.
