The short version

We do not collect your data. Synapsis AI runs entirely on your device. The app has no accounts, no server, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and makes no network calls in its learning features.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect, receive, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. Content you create (lesson materials, practice sets, scanned images, progress) is stored only on your device and never leaves it through the app.

On-device processing

All features — including document scanning (OCR), handwriting input, question generation, and learning games — are processed locally on your device using Apple's on-device frameworks. Images and text you scan are processed on-device and are not uploaded anywhere.

Audience

This release of Synapsis AI is designed for adults 18 and older (parents, teachers, tutors, and homeschool educators). It does not offer child accounts or child profiles, and it does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children — because it collects no information at all.

Device backups

App data is stored locally and excluded from device backups by default, so your content is not replicated to cloud backups by the app.

Deleting your data

Use Settings → Reset in the app to erase all locally stored data, or delete the app from your device — either removes everything, because no copy exists anywhere else.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and integrates no third-party AI services in this release.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes any of the above (for example, an optional cloud feature), we will update this policy before that version is released and describe exactly what changes.
